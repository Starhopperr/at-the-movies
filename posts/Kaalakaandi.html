Overview

Director: Akshat Verma

Cast: Saif Ali Khan, Deepak Dobriyal, Vijay Raaz, Sobhita Dhulipala

Genre: Dark Comedy, Crime, Thriller

Release Date: January 12, 2018

Plot Summary

When an honest and reserved man, Rileen (Saif Ali Khan), finds out he has terminal cancer, he decides to live his last moments doing everything he has never tried before. This leads to a wild night full of drugs, bizarre encounters, and chaos in the streets of Mumbai.

Review

*Kaalakaandi* is an unconventional dark comedy that blends multiple storylines, each exploring themes of fate, morality, and spontaneity. Saif Ali Khan delivers a standout performance as a man experiencing a psychedelic awakening, and the supporting cast adds depth to the unpredictable narrative. While the movie’s humor and eccentricity may not be for everyone, fans of experimental cinema will appreciate its quirky charm.

Final Verdict

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

Watch it if: You enjoy dark humor, offbeat storytelling, and strong performances.

Skip it if: You prefer straightforward narratives and dislike unconventional comedy.
